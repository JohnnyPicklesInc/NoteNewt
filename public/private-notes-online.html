🦎 Note Newt

Private notes online

Note Newt is a place to keep private notes online without handing your thoughts to an ad-profiling giant. Every note is encrypted with AES-256 on your device before it's stored, and you can start with no signup at all.

Write a private note →

Two levels of privacy

Default: your notes are encrypted and only you can read them in the app; we hold a key so that a lost device can still recover your notes after you sign in.

Zero-knowledge: add a passphrase and your encryption key is derived from it and never leaves your device. At that point we cannot read your notes even if we wanted to — the server only ever stores unreadable ciphertext.

Private by design

  • No third-party trackers or analytics scripts.
  • A strict content-security-policy — no foreign JavaScript runs next to your notes.
  • Works offline as an installable app.
