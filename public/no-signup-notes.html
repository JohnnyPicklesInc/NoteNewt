Why no signup matters

  • Instant. Capture a thought before it's gone — no forms in the way.

What about my other devices?

When you're ready, add a passwordless account with a one-tap email link and your notes sync to every device. You can even turn on a zero-knowledge passphrase so not even we can read them. Signing in is optional — the app works great without it.
